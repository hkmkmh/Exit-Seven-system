The Traffic Management System is designed to monitor and manage traffic flow at key exit points. By providing real-time data on vehicle counts, gate statuses, and traffic conditions, it helps ensure smooth and safe traffic management across the city. The system integrates advanced data analytics to detect traffic congestion and suggest possible solutions to ease the flow.

In addition to managing traffic at exit points, the system offers predictive analytics that allows authorities to plan and execute effective traffic control strategies. By utilizing historical data and real-time inputs, it can predict traffic patterns and inform gate operators on when to adjust flow or close certain lanes, ensuring a safe and efficient flow of vehicles.
